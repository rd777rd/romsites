The web development process starts with Planning and Requirements Gathering. We define the project's scope, objectives, and requirements. This involves detailed planning sessions to ensure we understand all technical and functional needs.

Finally, we proceed with Deployment and Launch. After thorough testing and approval, we deploy the website to the production environment. We provide post-launch support to address any issues and ensure a smooth go-live.
